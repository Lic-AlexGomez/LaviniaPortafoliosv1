ABOUT ME

I am a dedicated Pattern Maker and Apparel Professional with over 5 years of experience in the textile and fashion industry, specializing in pattern making, garment construction, and technical design.

My expertise lies in creating precise and innovative patterns, utilizing advanced CAD tools, and developing 3D apparel simulations. I have a strong foundation in both fashion design and protective clothing, blending creativity with functionality to deliver high-quality results.

My journey began in the fashion industry as a freelance graphic designer. After obtaining a bachelor's degree in graphic design, I moved on to pursue a career in pattern making and garment construction. I have worked on various projects, from designing simple patterns to creating intricate textile designs. My passion for pattern making and garment construction has led me to develop a strong foundation in both fashion design and protective clothing.

Throughout my career, I have contributed to numerous successful projects, from designing complex patterns to optimizing garment production processes. My work has consistently been recognized for its accuracy, efficiency, and adherence to quality standards. Currently, I am pursuing a PhD focused on sustainability and upcycling in fashion, where I am exploring innovative ways to integrate eco-friendly practices into the apparel industry. I am eager to bring my skills and passion for sustainable fashion to a forward-thinking company, where I can contribute to innovative design solutions and continue to grow as a professional. Known for my collaborative spirit and problem-solving abilities, I am committed to creating apparel that not only meets functional needs but also positively impacts the environment.
